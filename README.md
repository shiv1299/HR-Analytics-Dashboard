HR Analytics Dashboard – Project Description 📊

This project focuses on analyzing employee data to understand attrition trends, workforce distribution, and key HR metrics. The dashboard was built using Microsoft Power BI to transform raw HR data into meaningful insights that help organizations make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard provides an overview of employee statistics such as total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average age, salary, and average years at the company. It also includes multiple visualizations to analyze employee attrition based on education, age group, salary range, job role, and years at the company.

Interactive filters allow users to explore data by department (Human Resources, Research & Development, and Sales), making it easier to identify patterns and factors influencing employee turnover.

Key Insights from the Dashboard 📈

Total Employees: 1470

Attrition Count: 237

Attrition Rate: 16.1%

Highest attrition occurs in the 26–35 age group.

Employees earning below 5k salary show the highest attrition.

Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have the highest attrition among job roles.

Most employees who leave the company have 1–3 years of experience.
